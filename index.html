About
IPS

A consulting practice that takes products from concept to live system.

Imagine Peak Studios is directed by Matthew Hartley. We take products from concept to live system — direction, design, and AI engineering under one roof.

When it helps, we bring original productions: music, video, and sonic identity that turn a product into an experience. Current work includes Harmonic Wave (an AI-native media platform), Matthew Hartley Music (an artist platform), and RoboticWorks (compliance infrastructure for autonomous systems).
